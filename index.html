the best places to travel to

jaipur

I visited Jaipur in 2019

Jaipur is known is the pink city. But that's not all there is to it. One of the most colourful places I have ever seen, its rich and vibrant colours are part of the hustle-bustle of the market-vendors, auto-drivers, etc.



paris

I visited paris in 2014

Paris is known for its charms and cheese. But there are innumerable monuments in the city itself. The Arc de Triomphe, the Louvre and of course the Eiffel Tower. Featuring beautiful scenery and wonderful people, Paris certainly is a must to visit!



new york city

i visited new york city in 2017

One of the largest metropolises in the world, the people of New York City certainly reflect that! the entire city is skyscrapers and apartment complexes. But that doesn't mean it doesn't have its own charms!



review on the movies

AVENGERS: ENDGAME
Excellent film to culminate MCU ride

REVIEW OF THE MOVIE The ending to the succesful "AVENGERS" franchise, it was a great ending and closed much of the plotlines and cliffhangers. It felt like an information dump at times, but all in all a great movie.

LORD OF THE RINGS: RETURN OF THE KING
Excellent film to culminate MCU ride

REVIEW OF THE MOVIE The finale to the Lord of the Rings trilogy, this installment is the most epic movie of all time. You really get the sense that the incredibly complex world of J.R.R Tolkien exists. Props to the props makers.

news about environment

  • HEADING

    The Australian government has decided to declare koalas as an endangered species after the bushfire crisis. The bushfire crises killed many people, burned over 10 million hectares of land, destroyed over 2,000 homes and pushed many species towards extinction. It has been reported that over 1.25 billion animals were dead due to bushfires. Australian government committed A$50 million for emergency wildlife recovery program.

  • HEADING

    India, for the first time, was ranked among the top ten in the Climate Change Performance Index, CCPI, which goes further to prove that all efforts and activities being currently undertaken by the government are setting a tone of vast improvements. Government aimed to increase the share of non-fossil fuels to 175 GigaWatt by 2022 and further take it to 450 GW.
